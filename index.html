     Her Story

Serena has dedicated her life to building memories in peoples lives for over a decade.  She has a passion for people, culture, floral, and event design. Serena grew up in New York City where she has worked in a variety of industries and it is these experiences that has cultivated exceptional planning and management abilities while maintaining meticulous attention to detail and presentation.  She believes creating quality event packages for her clients is a easier way to get the job done affectively, care free, time saving and affordable for her clients.  It is her job to ensure each of those goals are met.  To ensure the best services for her clients,  Serena continues to educate herself on cultural traditions and etiquette.  Parallel to that knowledge is constant research to keep abreast of innovative event planning trends and tools.  Serena is a multi- tasked dedicated mother of three who also contributes to her community by empowering young women as a public speaker and mentor.  In 2013 Serena opened her show room in the Hudson Valley offering a distinctive service of a full service location that sets her apart from others.
